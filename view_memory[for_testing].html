#01 Carla

My mother and brother nearly died in a walking accident when I was around 3. I have a strong memory of my father telling me to ‘stay here’ in a tone he hadn’t used before, as he ran back to rescue both of them. I had a tendency to walk ahead and daydream when I was a child, later on I had a police helicopter after me as I cycled 6 miles away from my family, around the age of 8 or 9.
