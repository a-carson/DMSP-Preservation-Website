We are DMSP Preservation team 2020, studying a Digital Media Studio Project module at the Edinburgh College of Art. We came together from a variety of visual, sonic, and digital design backgrounds to explore the theme of ‚ÄòPreservation‚Äô in the digital age. From the outset we were fascinated by the process of memory and the role of dreaming in the formation of memory, and how much our memories are seemingly and endlessly recorded on social media platforms. We developed the character, IAIN, who is the interface of an hypothetical archive in the future and who features in the opening animation for the website. His role as the interface is not simply to record memories in a straight forward way but he generates unique audio-visual pieces for each user depending on their memory-text.
